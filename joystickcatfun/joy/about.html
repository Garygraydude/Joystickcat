About JoystickCat

JoystickCat is an indie game studio passionate about creating fun, retro-inspired games. Our mission is to deliver engaging adventures, charming worlds, and unforgettable experiences for players around the world.

Our games range from challenging platformers like King Jump to story-driven RPGs such as The Summit series and Hostile Horizon. Each project is crafted with love for pixel art, great gameplay, and a splash of nostalgia.

Our Mission

To empower indie gaming by creating unique and accessible games for everyone who loves storytelling, creativity, and challenges.
